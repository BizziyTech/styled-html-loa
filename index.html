Annexure A – Job Duties

Job Title: Sales Manager and New Client Relationship Lead

Key Duties and Responsibilities:

  • New Business Development:
    • Identify, prospect, and acquire new clients to expand the company’s portfolio.
    • Conduct market research to identify potential business opportunities and industries.
    • Develop and execute outreach strategies to engage potential clients through networking, referrals, and direct sales.
    • Attend industry events and conferences to generate leads and build business relationships.
  • Client Relationship Management:
    • Build, nurture, and sustain strong relationships with both new and existing clients.
    • Serve as the primary point of contact for key clients, ensuring their satisfaction and retention.
    • Address client concerns promptly and effectively, fostering long-term partnerships.
    • Develop and maintain a comprehensive client onboarding process to ensure a seamless transition for new clients into service delivery.
  • Sales Strategy & Pipeline Management:
    • Develop and implement strategic sales plans to drive business growth.
    • Identify and pursue new business opportunities in alignment with company objectives.
    • Manage the sales pipeline, ensuring steady deal flow and conversion.
  • Proposal & Budget Preparation:
    • Prepare compelling sales proposals and budget forecasts tailored to client needs.
    • Collaborate with designers and operations teams to plan and execute successful campaigns.
  • Client Retention & Event Success Monitoring:
    • Oversee event execution, ensuring alignment with client expectations and company standards.
    • Monitor client satisfaction post-event and implement improvement strategies for future campaigns.
  • Cross-Departmental Collaboration:
    • Work closely with Operations and Finance teams to streamline internal processes.
    • Ensure smooth workflow and alignment between sales, finance, and execution teams.
  • Market Analysis & Competitive Positioning:
    • Stay updated on market trends, competitor activities, and client needs.
    • Provide insights and recommendations to refine sales strategies and enhance competitiveness.
    • Track and report on new business development activities and revenue growth performance.
